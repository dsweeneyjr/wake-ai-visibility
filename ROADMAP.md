# Wake Tech AI Search Intelligence
### Product Roadmap

---

## Vision

As AI-powered search becomes a primary way people discover colleges, Wake Tech needs a way to measure how AI platforms represent the college, identify opportunities to improve visibility, and monitor changes over time.

The goal is to provide actionable intelligence — not just analytics — to help Wake Tech make informed decisions using data.

## Phase 4 – Decision Intelligence

### AI Response Analysis

Automatically identify:

- Was Wake Tech recommended?
- Position in response
- Competitors
- Citations
- Visibility score

### Content Intelligence

Identify:

- Missing topics
- Weak pages
- Frequently cited competitors
- Citation trends

### Recommendations

Generate recommendations such as:

- Improve page content
- Expand FAQs
- Add career outcomes
- Improve structured data
- Strengthen internal linking

**Goal:** Help content teams prioritize improvements using data.
